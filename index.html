Good to Know

Policies

Payment

Payment is due at confirmation — Venmo or Cash App. Orders not paid by Friday will be released to waitlist.

Allergies

All items are baked in a home kitchen that uses nuts, dairy, eggs, and gluten. Please note any allergies when ordering.

Pickup Only

All orders are local pickup in Sturgis, MI. No delivery at this time. Location details sent with confirmation.

Limited Quantities

Everything is made in small batches. Once an item sells out for the week it's gone — first come, first served.

Custom Orders

Want something special? Leave a note in your order form or reach out directly. I love a challenge.

Cancellations

Life happens. Let me know before Friday and I'll do my best to work something out.
